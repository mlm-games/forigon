Launcher for Wear OS and full Android smartwatches.

Features:

- List or bubble cloud app drawer
- Virtual rotary bezel - scroll by dragging screen edge
- Quick settings panel for supported devices (flashlight, DND, WiFi, Bluetooth)
- App search with fuzzy matching
- Hide apps from drawer
- Adjustable UI scale for small screens
- Reduced motion mode for battery saving

Controls:

- Tap watch face → open apps
- Long-press watch face → settings
- Swipe up → apps
- Swipe down → quick settings
- Drag along screen edge → scroll or zoom

Supports round and square displays. Should also work with physical rotary bezels and crowns.
No plans of having watch faces or other external apis support for near future due to other tasks.
