Forigon is a open-source launcher for Android TV devices. It tries to provide a clean, and fast home screen experience without any big-banner advertisements optimized for remote control navigation.

<b>Features:</b>

• <b>TV-First Design</b> - For 10-foot UI with D-pad navigation and focus handling
• <b>App Organization</b> - Favorites and Hidden apps
• <b>Quick Access Sidebar</b> - Expandable sidebar with clock
• <b>Recent Apps</b> - Quickly access your most recently used apps

<b>Privacy Focused:</b>

• No ads or tracking
• No internet permission required
• No data collection
• Fully open source

<b>Requirements:</b>

• Android TV or device with Leanback support
• Android 7.0 (API 24) or higher

Forigon aims to be a dizziness causing alternative to stock Android TV launchers, while removing bloat or sponsored content.
If you would prefer a simpler looking launcher, would recommend LeanBackOnFire instead.
